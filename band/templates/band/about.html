Our Story

Circle of Bosses is not just a hip-hop group; it's a movement, a collective of talented individuals brought together by their love for music and their desire to make an impact on the Cape Town hip-hop scene. Formed in the vibrant streets of Cape Town, South Africa, Circle of Bosses is a fusion of creativity, storytelling, and lyrical prowess. Comprising four distinct talents - Meezy Maps, CJ, Young Eddy, and Glimpse - each member brings a unique flavor to the group, creating a dynamic synergy that sets them apart. Our music is more than just beats and rhymes; it's a reflection of the realities, dreams, and aspirations of the streets we call home. From sold-out gigs to chart-topping tracks, Circle of Bosses has rapidly risen to prominence, captivating audiences with their electrifying performances and socially conscious lyrics. We invite you to join us on this musical journey as we continue to push the boundaries of hip-hop, one verse at a time. Circle of Bosses is more than a name; it's a promise of innovative, authentic, and soul-stirring music that resonates with the heart of Cape Town.
